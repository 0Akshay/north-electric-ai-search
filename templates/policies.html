Company Policies

Our policies ensure reliable service, customer satisfaction, and regulatory compliance.

Service Connection Policy

Customers requesting new service must submit an application at least 2 business days before the desired start date. North Electric reserves the right to verify identity and property access before connection.

Disconnection Policy

Accounts with unpaid balances over 30 days past due are subject to disconnection. Customers will receive at least one written notice before disconnection occurs.

Privacy Policy

We protect customer data according to national and state regulations. Personal information will never be shared without consent, except when required by law.

Green Energy Commitment

North Electric is committed to sourcing 50% of its energy from renewable sources by 2030 and achieving net-zero emissions by 2050.
