Billing & Payment Information

North Electric offers flexible and convenient billing options to suit your needs. Below you can learn about payment methods, due dates, and how to manage your account.

Your bill is generated once a month based on your meter readings. Payment is due within 21 days of the bill issue date. Late payments may incur a 1.5% fee on the outstanding balance.

üí≥ Payment Options

  • Online Portal: Log in to the Customer Portal to pay using your credit/debit card or bank account.

  • Mobile App: Use the North Electric app to view and pay bills on the go.
  • By Mail: Send checks or money orders to North Electric Billing Dept., P.O. Box 1234, Springfield, USA.
  • In Person: Pay at any authorized payment center or North Electric office.
